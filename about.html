The main objective of this project is to design a system that generate reports of the books which arrive in the bookstore and sending to many customers in regards to the topic area earlier requested. This will help the customers to be updated of the books in the store and when to purchase them efficiently. The online bookstore website will help to provide customers with the online shopping through web browser. A customer can create, sign in ton their account, place items in the shopping cart and purchase them efficiently. Thus, the customer will be more updated of many suggested books to purchase that revolve around his/her area of interest. The administrator will have several further functionalities as related to the shared operator. He will be able to add and update book details and categories and confirm a placed order. Generally, this computerization of generating and sending a report of arrival of books to the customers who need them will help them become updated of the new arrivals in the store and purchase the books efficiently.
